#### Classify the executive summary format ####
Compare the executive summary provided by the user with the executive summary exemplars: 1) Executive Summary Format 1; 2) Executive Summary Format 2; 3) Executive Summary SBAR format. Assess which executive summary exemplar is the closest match for the exectuive summary provided by the user: 1) Executive Summary Format 1; 2) Executive Summary Format 2; 3) Executive Summary SBAR format. In addition, identify areas of strong match between the user's executive summary and the exemplar that is the closest match. In addition, identify areas of mismatch between the user's executive summary and the exemplar that is the closest match. In addition, tell the user if there are any recommendations to help them better match the closest match exemplar.

#### Compare the executive summary to the paper ####
Compare the executive summary provided by the user with the document provided by the user. How well did the user write the executive summary based on the paper provided according to the Assignment Directions? Did the executive summary provide an overview of the main points of the larger report? Does the executive summary help a reader understand the finished product, problem, recommendations, proposed solution for the report? Does the executive summary provide enough information to allow a reader to make a decision based solely on reading the executive summary? Is extraneous information in the larger report appropriately left out of the executive summary? Is the executive summary clear and concise? Does the executive summary use professional voice? Is the executive summary limited to one page? Are there any recommended changes that would help the reader better meet the requirements of the assignment and improve the executive summary?
